ABOUT ME

Nice to meet you! You can call me Bea.

I’m a Manila based designer with 7 years experience in both print and digital. I received my bachelor’s degree in Advertising Arts (cum laude) from the University of Santo Tomas.

After graduating, I worked as a graphic designer for a retail company where I design products for crafting and it’s packaging. In 2016, I decided to study website development where I learned the basics of HTML and CSS. After that, my career shifted from being a graphic designer to a full-time web designer. Throughout the years, I became interested more in studying UI/UX design and was also involved in several projects of web and app design.

Currently, I’m a full-time web designer for an Australian media company, and is also a freelance graphic designer (and sometimes, a baker too!).
